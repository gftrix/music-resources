# Guitar Learning Resources & Profiles

## FaChords Homepage
**URL:** https://www.fachords.com/  
**Description:**  
FaChords Guitar is an educational platform created by Giancarlo Facoetti offering free and premium resources for learning guitar. It includes interactive tools, lessons, theory tutorials, ebooks, and training resources focused on chords, scales, fretboard knowledge, songwriting, and improvisation. 

---

## FaChords Guitar Chords
**URL:** https://www.fachords.com/guitar-chord/  
**Description:**  
An interactive guitar chord reference featuring chord diagrams, chord formulas, interval construction, voicings, inversions, and chord-building theory. Includes beginner open chords, bar chords, power chords, and advanced harmonic concepts. 

---

## FaChords Guitar Scales
**URL:** https://www.fachords.com/guitar-scale/  
**Description:**  
A complete guitar scales learning hub with an interactive scale finder, scale diagrams, interval formulas, arpeggios, fretboard patterns, and guidance for applying scales to improvisation and soloing. Includes box patterns, 3-notes-per-string systems, and theory explanations.

---

## FaChords Fretboard Guide
**URL:** https://www.fachords.com/fretboard-chord-tone-map/  
**Description:**  
A guide to understanding the guitar fretboard through chord-tone maps and interval visualization. Helps players learn note relationships, create chord voicings, explore inversions, and connect theory to practical fretboard navigation. 

**Related Tool (Interactive Trainer):**  
https://www.fachords.com/tools/fretboard-trainer/  
Interactive fretboard memorization trainer for note recognition and fretboard fluency. 

---

## FaChords Chord Progressions
**URL:** https://www.fachords.com/common-chord-progressions/  
**Description:**  
A practical collection of common and advanced chord progressions, including I-IV-V, I-V-vi-IV, ii-V-I, modal progressions, substitutions, and songwriting-oriented harmonic movement. Includes examples, theory breakdowns, and genre-based applications.
